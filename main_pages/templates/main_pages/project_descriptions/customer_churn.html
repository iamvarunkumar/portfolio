Customer Churn Prediction

Developed a customer churn prediction model to reduce churn rate and improve customer retention by employing survival analysis techniques and integrating an LLM for personalized explanations.

Key Responsibilities and Achievements

  • **Churn Reduction:** Developed a customer churn prediction model that reduced churn rate by 0%, directly contributing to revenue enhancement.
  • **Survival Analysis:** Employed survival analysis using Cox proportional hazards regression with Box-Cox transformations to model customer lifecycles and identify key predictors of churn.
  • **LLM for Personalized Explanations:** Integrated an LLM (LangChain) to generate personalized explanations for high-risk customers, enabling targeted interventions and improving retention strategies.
  • **Deployment:** Deployed the churn prediction model and LLM-driven insights via a Flask web application, enabling proactive customer relationship management.

Technologies Used

Flask LangChain Cox proportional hazards regression Box-Cox transformations

Project Details

The Customer Churn Prediction project focused on developing a model to predict customer churn and implement strategies to improve customer retention. This involved:

  • **Churn Reduction:** The primary goal was to build a model that could accurately predict which customers were at high risk of churning, allowing for proactive interventions to prevent them from leaving. The successful implementation of this model resulted in a 15% reduction in churn rate, directly contributing to increased revenue.
  • **Survival Analysis:** To effectively model customer lifecycles and understand the factors that influence churn, survival analysis techniques were employed. Specifically, Cox proportional hazards regression was used, along with Box-Cox transformations to ensure the data met the model's assumptions. This approach provided insights into the time-to-churn and the relative importance of different predictors.
  • **LLM for Personalized Explanations:** To enhance customer relationship management, an LLM (LangChain) was integrated into the system. This allowed for the generation of personalized explanations for high-risk customers. These explanations provided insights into the reasons for their potential churn, enabling targeted communication and interventions to address their specific concerns and improve their likelihood of staying.
  • **Deployment:** The churn prediction model and the LLM-driven insights were deployed via a Flask web application. This application provided a user-friendly interface for customer relationship managers to access and utilize the predictions and explanations in their daily work, facilitating proactive customer retention efforts.
